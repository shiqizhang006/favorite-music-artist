我想變做個木偶，我想變做個石頭，我想變做山丘，我想變做小狗。點先可以沒憂愁？點先可以沒淚流？點先可以沒內疚？點先可以自由？

Playing a mini Casio keyboard, My Little Airport creates a “quirky” style of arrangement that immediately catches me. Their lyrics are the most unique part, which overflows with unpolished emotions delivered by plain language. Their songs are as short as a playlet but always heart-touching. They are not only musicians, but also poets, and passive romanticists. 

This song is called "Let's Leave Together" ("我們一起離開吧"). Translation of the lyrics: "I want to turn into a puppet, I want to turn into a stone, I want to turn into a hill, I want to turn into a dog. How could we be rid of sorrows? How could we be rid of tears? How could we be rid of self guilt? How could we be free?"
